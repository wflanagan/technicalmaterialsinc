Mission Statement

Technical Materials mission is simple but not easy. We sell the best quality high temperature materials needed by our customers at the best price they can get to allow them to be competitive in their product area.

Company History Success Stories

Technical Materials, as a major supplier of refractory materials for the glass, steel and other industries requiring high temperature materials, is committed to providing our customers with the best products and consulting services.

We have provided the AZS working lining to The Cameron Family Glass Furnace in Kalama, WA, the largest electric glass furnace in the world, and our sources of AZS for glass furnaces have half the lead time than other AZS suppliers.

Technical Materials has also sold wear-resistant brick to be used as impact pads for steel furnaces. And we have sourced and sold carbon and graphite felts and boards for vacuum furnaces and heat treating furnaces.

Technical Materials consults with companies buying products from China or interested in manufacturing in China. This includes qualifying a joint venture company, evaluating a potential supplier and the supervision of the construction of a ceramic facility, or a new ceramic product line.
