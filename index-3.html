Technical Materials sells the best high temperature materials at the best price possible so our customers in the glass and steel industries can successfully compete in their product area.

Technical Materials Inc. imports and sells high temperature materials needed in North America. These include high temperature insulation, refractories, high temperature metals for use as heating elements and thermocouples, and any other high temperature materials needed for processing metals, glass and ceramic materials. Focusing primarily in the glass and steel industries, we have also sold products for many other high temperature applications, and we represent manufactures for AZS Refractories.
