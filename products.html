Tungsten and Molybdenum Products

Technical Materials represents Zhengzhou Chida Tungsten & Molybdenum Products Company. We manufacture both polished and unpolished electrodes for the glass industry that are made according to the customer's specifications. We also supply Molybdenum disilicide (MoSi2) heating elements for heat-treating furnaces, and we can also supply molybdenum powder, molybdenum plates and sheets, molybdenum piercing mandrels for the production of tubing, molybdenum rods and crucibles. Bottom line is: If it is made of molybdenum, we can supply it.

Technical Materials can also supply tungsten electrodes, tungsten wire, tungsten screws, tungsten brackets and high density tungsten alloys. If you need any tungsten products, let us give you a price quotation.

Steelmaking Products

Technical Materials supplies steel-making refractories from several Chinese manufacturers. These products include magnesia brick, magnesia carbon brick, magnesia chrome brick, magnesia dolomite block, special bottom pour shapes, silica brick and monolithic silica and magnesia refractories.

We also supply graphite electrodes, structural graphite shapes and isostatic graphite.

Carbon Felt and Graphite felt

Technical Materials represents Beijing Great Wall Company, the largest and highest manufacturer of carbon and graphite felts in China. Their precuts are the standard in China and are an exceptional value everywhere in the world. Give us a call for price and availability.

Honeycomb Ceramics

Technical Materials supplies honeycomb ceramics for use in regenerators, metal filtration and air filtration. We supply various compositions and many different sizes. Whatever your honeycomb ceramic need, give us a call. You will be pleasantly surprised.
